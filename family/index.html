Parental Leave Policies

Parental leave policies vary across the business world. Some companies offer paid parental leave while others offer unpaid parental leave. The duration of parental leave also varies from company to company. In some countries, the government mandates a minimum number of weeks of parental leave that companies must offer to their employees. For example, in the United States, there is no federal law mandating paid parental leave. However, some states have passed laws mandating paid parental leave for employees.
